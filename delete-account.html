Delete Your Account

Igbo Community Hull (ICH)

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your ICH account and associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from the App

You can delete your account directly within the ICH app:

  1. Open the ICH app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your decision

Option 2: Request via Email

Send an email to request account deletion:

Request Account Deletion

Email: support@igbocommunityhull.com

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your address and personal details
  • Your profile photo
  • Your forum posts and comments
  • Your event RSVPs
  • Your business directory listings
  • Your family account links

What Data May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes:

  • Financial records: Payment history and receipts (retained for 7 years for tax/legal compliance)
  • Attendance records: Historical meeting attendance (anonymised)
  • Aggregated data: Anonymous statistics that cannot identify you

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will need to create a new account if you wish to rejoin the community.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, please contact us at:
support@igbocommunityhull.com
